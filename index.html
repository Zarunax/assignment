ABOUT ME

Who am I?

Hi there! I'm Zarunax, although some of you may know me as Josh. I am a part-time, small streamer based in the East of England, and I go live each week over at https://twitch.tv/zarunax, where you can find me playing a mixture of games. Whether I am building badly and dying a lot in survival games, getting confused and failing at story games, or even getting made to scream by my chat using jumpscares during horror games, you can be sure of one thing - I will always try to make it fun, lighthearted and welcoming.
